Our mission is to create a world-wide repository of wild mushroom sightings. Using the humanity's decades of cumulative expertise we aim to gather additional information on the biodiversity of Fungus occuring on our natural habitat, planet Earth. This data collection will be used to generate supporting information for discovery of new mushroom species.

How it Works

We’ve designed a simple, efficient process for our users to gather mushroon sighting information. Here’s how it works.

01

Make Observation

We often encounter Fungus in our day-to-day travels that is easily overlooked and or ignored. A simple observation can be made by snapping a picture of the Fungus occuring in its natural habitat.

02

Identify Fungus

Using the information held within this website and available to us on the world wide web (we recommend Wikipedia) attempt to identify the mushroom observed in step 1.

03

Create Sighting

Catalog the mushroom sighting through our application and provide location, image url, and taxonomic information.
